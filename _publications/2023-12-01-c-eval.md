C-Eval is a comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite designed to assess the advanced knowledge and reasoning abilities of foundation models in the Chinese language context. The benchmark covers 52 diverse disciplines across four difficulty levels, providing a multi-level and multi-discipline evaluation framework for measuring progress in Chinese-language AI capabilities.
