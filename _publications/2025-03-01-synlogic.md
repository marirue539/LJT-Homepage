We propose SynLogic, a method for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale to enable training of large language models on logical reasoning tasks. The approach generates diverse, well-formed reasoning traces that can be automatically verified for correctness, providing high-quality training signal for improving the reasoning capabilities of language models beyond what is achievable with non-verifiable training data.
