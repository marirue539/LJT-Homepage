We present a method for composing parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations, enabling flexible and modular adaptation of large pre-trained models. By treating adapter modules as composable elements that can be combined through arithmetic operations, we demonstrate significant improvements in multi-task and cross-domain adaptation scenarios.
